My Story


My name is Michael C. Calhoun. I'm a systems analyst from Manhattan, KS. I grew up in Northeastern Pennsylvania, in the Poconos Mountains. I lived in a small town called Jim Thorpe, PA. I joined the U.S Army when I was 17 and went to Ft. Jackson S.C. for my Basic Combat Training, and then to Ft. Gordon Georgia for my Advanced Individual Training in radio and satellite operation and maintenance.Tweets by @Mikey_Calhoun My first duty station was Fort Riley, KS. I served with the 1st Infantry Division's Combat Aviation Brigade. I worked for the brigade's IT Help Desk, serving in such roles as; network security administrator and systems administration. While on Active Duty I was able to study independently to attain several IT certifications such as CompTIA A+ and Security+, and Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist. I was also able to attain my Associates of Science degree in Computer Science, graduating as a Phi Theta Kappa honor graduate.

I was awarded a slot in the Kansas State Army ROTC Green to Gold program in December 2012 to train to become an United States Army Officer. I currently serve as a Lead Information Technology Consultant at Kansas State University's Information Technology Assistance Center. For a full listing of my abilites and job expereiences, please feel free to download my PDF resume.

I am married to an amazing woman named Katherine. I live with her and an adorable rabbit Nelly, and an anxious cat named Dakota in Manhattan.
